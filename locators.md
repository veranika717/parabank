# Task #3 – Locators

## Common part: Parabank

**Username field**
- CSS: `input[name='username']`
- XPath: `//input[@name='username']`

**Password field**
- CSS: `input[name='password']`
- XPath: `//input[@name='password']`

**REGISTER button**
- CSS: `input[value='Register']`
- XPath: `//input[@value='Register']`

**Contact link**
- CSS: `a[href='contact.htm']`
- XPath: `//a[@href='contact.htm']`

**PARA BANK logo**
- CSS: `img.logo`
- XPath: `//img[@class='logo']`
